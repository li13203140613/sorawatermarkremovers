## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Do invitation codes expire?

Usually invitation codes have no time limit, but have usage count limits. They become invalid once the limit is reached.

### Q2: Can one account use multiple invitation codes?

No. Each account can only be activated once and cannot change or stack invitation codes after activation.

### Q3: Why does my invitation code show as invalid?

Possible reasons:

1. The code has been fully used
2. Input has spaces or wrong case
3. Your account is already activated
4. Network delay, try again later

### Q4: How many videos can I generate after activation?

Free accounts usually have daily limits, specific credits depend on:

- Account type (free/paid)
- Server load
- Your usage history

### Q5: When will Sora 2 be fully open?

OpenAI hasn't announced a specific timeline, expected to open to public around mid-2025.
